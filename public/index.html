HCAI Compliance Report — Valley Medical Center Expansion
Critical
RULE-003 · Infection Control

Isolation Room Negative Pressure Deficiency

Isolation rooms in Occupied Hospital must maintain negative air pressure per ASHRAE 170 Table 7.1. Drawings do not indicate directional airflow or pressure differential monitoring for Room 214-A.

High
RULE-007 · MEP / Electrical

Emergency Power Transfer Timing Not Documented

Life safety systems in an Occupied Hospital require automatic transfer to emergency power within 10 seconds per NFPA 99 and Title 24 Part 3. Specification Section 16480 does not address transfer time.

Medium
RULE-011 · Seismic

Seismic Bracing for Medical Gas Piping

Seismic Zone D project requires lateral bracing for all medical gas lines > 2" diameter per OSHPD SB-99-04. Bracing schedule missing from Mechanical sheets M-3.1 through M-3.4.
